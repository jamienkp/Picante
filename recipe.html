Alouda Sorbet Recipe

A refreshing and creamy drink perfect for warm days.

Ingredients

  • 7 g Agar agar
  • 200 ml Water
  • 1 tbsp Raw sugar
  • 3 tbsp Casa-casa (basil seeds) or chia seeds
  • 375 ml Condensed milk
  • 750 ml Milk
  • ¼ tsp Almond essence
  • Rose pink food coloring

Instructions

A day prior...
  1. Place agar agar in a saucepan with 200ml cold water and let it settle for 2 hours.
  2. Heat over medium heat, stirring until agar agar dissolves completely.
  3. Add 2 tbsp raw sugar and bring to a boil until the sugar fully dissolves.
  4. Transfer to a dish, let it cool, and refrigerate overnight until it sets.
Cooking day...
  1. Shred the agar agar mousse using a grater and set aside.
  2. Place basil seeds in a bowl, remove bad seeds, and soak in 8 cups of cold water for 30 minutes.
  3. Once seeds expand, stir in condensed milk, milk, and agar agar mousse gratings.
  4. Add almond essence and 3 drops of rose pink food coloring, then mix well.
  5. Refrigerate and serve chilled over ice.
